The World

Bayouboujee. The Scarlet Elixir. A city that never lets you go.

Baron Vieux built a restaurant out of a shipping container and a stubborn refusal to rent what he could own. Olivia Marigny sang her way into his life the same way she sings into every room — without trying, and without apology.

What started as one book has become a series: the story of how Bayouboujee was built, the story of who Baron was before it, and the story that's still being written.

"Whatever I build, I'm going to own. You can't double the rent on something you own."
