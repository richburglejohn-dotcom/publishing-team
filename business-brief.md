# Business Brief

## Creator
Lejohn Richburg III / FuturIQ

## Current Books
- **Published:** Culinary Crescendo: A Symphony of Love in the Big Easy
- **In Progress (Prequel):** Culinary Crescendo: The Scarlet Foundation / Satire of New Orleans

## Genre
Culinary fiction / romance / New Orleans noir

## Key Characters
- **Baron Vieux** — sous chef, entrepreneur, storyteller. Builds Bayouboujee from a shipping container.
- **Olivia** — blues singer, tarot reader, Virgin Islands roots. Co-creator of Bayouboujee.

## Signature Story Elements
- Bayouboujee: a raw, stripped-down shipping container restaurant concept
- The Scarlet Elixir: Baron's proprietary sauce reduction — his most guarded creation
- The Gulf Remedy: signature spice blend and crab starter dish
- The Scarlet Redfish: Baron's flagship dish
- New Orleans setting: French Quarter, Magazine Street, WWOZ, dive bars, tarot, blues

## Tone & Voice
Dark, elegant, cinematic. Victorian-noir meets culinary hustle. "Premium, practical, no fluff."

## Audience
- Readers of culinary fiction and Southern romance
- New Orleans culture enthusiasts
- Fans of strong, entrepreneurial protagonists
- Book clubs and literary fiction readers

## The Bigger Vision: Baron Vieux as Brand Entity

**The books are only the beginning.**

Baron Vieux is being developed as a trademark and intellectual property — a character that lives across multiple media formats. Think of how Colonel Sanders became a brand, or how a hip-hop artist's persona becomes an empire. Baron Vieux is that kind of character.

**The IP Expansion Roadmap:**

| Phase | Medium | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 (Now) | Books | Establish the world, characters, and voice. Build the reader base. |
| Phase 2 (Next) | Music | Baron's hip-hop roots + Olivia's blues/jazz create a natural music extension |
| Phase 3 | Video | YouTube, music videos, short films, documentary-style content |
| Phase 4 | Products | The Scarlet Elixir as a real product? Bayouboujee as a real brand? |
| Phase 5 | Trademark | Baron Vieux as fully protected intellectual property and licensing entity |

## Business Goal
- Finish and publish the prequel (The Scarlet Foundation)
- Cross-market the prequel to existing readers of the first book
- Build an audience and email list around the Culinary Crescendo series
- Establish Baron Vieux as a recognizable brand entity beyond the books
- Expand FuturIQ into AI-assisted publishing and creator education
