# Character Portrait Prompt — Rampart

*Built to match the established visual style of the series (Baron Vieux storyboards,
`prequel-cover-prompt.md`, the Scarlet Elixir video): photorealistic, cinematic,
warm amber/candlelight palette, Victorian-noir gaslamp aesthetic meets French
Quarter kitchen grit.*

**Source:** Character built out in `references/series-bible.md` and physically
described in Chapter 1 of *The Scarlet Foundation* (`outputs/edited-chapters-1-9.md`).
Lead cook, Baron Vieux's second-in-command. Named for Rampart Street.

---

## Primary Prompt (paste into Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Ideogram, Flow, or similar)

```
A cinematic, photorealistic portrait of a New Orleans line cook, shot like a
film still, warm amber and candlelight tones with deep shadows, Victorian-noir
gaslamp aesthetic mixed with French Quarter kitchen grit.

SUBJECT: A Black man in his late 30s to 40s, built like a shipping container —
broad, heavyset shoulders, powerfully built, roughly 260 lbs of solid muscle.
Bald head, clean-shaven or close-cropped facial hair, dark skin with the
particular sheen of hours spent over high heat. His expression carries
controlled intensity — a man used to running multiple stations at once,
focused and unshaken, but with a flicker of guardedness behind the eyes, like
someone who keeps a wall up out of habit.

WARDROBE: Kitchen whites or a dark chef's coat, sleeves pushed up to the
forearms, a well-worn apron tied with practiced efficiency. No embroidery —
simpler and more work-worn than Baron Vieux's coat, befitting a lead cook
rather than the sous chef running the whole kitchen.

SETTING: A professional restaurant kitchen at night, warm overhead pendant
lighting, exposed brick, stainless steel and open flame just out of focus
behind him, steam faintly visible. Same kitchen world as the rest of the
Culinary Crescendo series — moody, atmospheric, high detail.

FRAMING: Medium portrait, shoulders and up, three-quarter angle or straight
to camera, shallow depth of field with the kitchen glowing softly behind him.

Color palette: scarlet red (#a6182c), gaslamp amber/gold (#c9973f), deep
charcoal black (#0d0b0a), parchment cream (#ede4d3) for any text elements.
Fully photorealistic, no cartoon or illustration style, shot like a film
still, 8k, dramatic lighting.

Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait / character reference proportions)
```

---

## Notes for whoever generates this

- **Build is the most important detail to get right** — Rampart reads as "the
  defensive wall of the kitchen," physically and emotionally. The prompt leans
  hard on shipping-container-broad shoulders and bulk; if the output comes back
  lean or slight, it's off-model. Regenerate rather than accept a smaller build.
- **Bald head, dark skin, no glasses** — this is the key visual differentiator
  from Baron Vieux (who is lean, sharp-featured, and always in his signature
  square black-framed glasses). Rampart should read as a distinct, separate
  presence even in a lineup.
- **Expression is the emotional core.** He's not scowling and he's not soft —
  the brief is controlled intensity with a guarded flicker underneath. If the
  first pass reads as either "angry" or "friendly," it's missed the mark.
- **Reference the Baron Vieux art** (`PrequelFrontCover.jpg`, the storyboard
  panels, or the Scarlet Elixir video stills) if your tool supports image-to-image
  or style reference — matching the exact amber/candlelight grade and kitchen
  environment ties this directly into the established world.

## Optional Alternate — "The Wall Up" Variant

For a second portrait capturing his flare-up state (referenced in Chapter 3 —
"the defensive wall shot up instantly"), swap the expression line for:

```
His jaw is set, eyes sharp with sudden defensive intensity — a man mid-flare,
tongs gripped tight in one hand, caught in the half-second before he catches
himself and pulls the wall back down.
```

This pairs well as a two-image set (calm precision / defensive flare) the
same way Baron's early storyboards used contrasting panels to show range.
