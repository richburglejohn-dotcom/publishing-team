# Prequel Cover Art Prompt — The Scarlet Foundation

*Built to match the visual style of the Book 1 cover (`CulinaryCrescendo_frontcover_google.pdf`): photorealistic, cinematic, warm amber/candlelight palette, split-scene composition, glowing embossed gold title typography, Victorian-noir meets New Orleans French Quarter atmosphere.*

---

## Primary Prompt (paste into Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Ideogram, or similar)

```
A cinematic, photorealistic book cover for a New Orleans culinary fiction novel,
split composition, night scene, warm amber and candlelight tones with deep
shadows, Victorian-noir gaslamp aesthetic mixed with French Quarter grit.

LEFT SIDE: A vintage 1990s French Quarter coffee shop storefront at night,
"KALDI'S" glowing in warm neon-style signage above tall street-facing windows,
exposed brick and string lights, a teenage Black boy in an apron standing
behind a large vintage espresso machine, steam rising, focused expression,
warm interior lighting spilling onto the wet cobblestone sidewalk outside.

RIGHT SIDE: A modern professional restaurant kitchen at night, dramatic
overhead pendant lighting, stainless steel and open flame, a young adult
Black male chef in a dark apron plating a red sauce reduction over seared
fish with intense focus, steam and light catching the plate, a
Victorian-noir elegance to his posture — sharp-featured, square black
glasses, composed.

Between the two scenes: a antique brass ornate divider or wrought-iron
fleur-de-lis motif, tying the two eras together visually.

Bottom foreground: a small stack of objects suggesting time passing — a
worn leather knife roll, a lit candle, a vintage coffee cup beside a chef's
folded apron.

Title treatment: large elegant gold embossed serif typography reading
"CULINARY CRESCENDO" at the top, glowing subtly like gaslight, with a
smaller italic script subtitle beneath reading "The Scarlet Foundation" —
same font family and glow treatment as a Playfair Display serif with warm
amber backlight. Author credit "By Lejohn Richburg III" in clean small
caps at the bottom.

Color palette: scarlet red (#a6182c), gaslamp amber/gold (#c9973f), deep
charcoal black (#0d0b0a), parchment cream (#ede4d3) for text. Moody,
atmospheric, high detail, shot like a film still, 8k, dramatic lighting,
no cartoon or illustration style — fully photorealistic.

Aspect ratio: 2:3 (standard book cover proportions)
```

---

## Notes for whoever generates this

- **Keep the split-scene structure** — it's the strongest visual link to Book 1's cover (which splits between Olivia performing and Baron cooking). Here it becomes "young Baron at Kaldi's" vs. "Baron the chef" — mirrors the book's before/after structure.
- **Match the text glow effect** exactly — Book 1's title has a warm gold gaslamp glow baked into the letters themselves, not just a solid gold color. Ask explicitly for "glowing embossed text" if the first output looks flat.
- **If the platform struggles with rendering the two-word title + subtitle cleanly** (common weak spot for AI image tools), generate the scene *without* text first, then add title typography separately in Canva, Photoshop, or Affinity Photo using a Playfair Display or similar elegant serif font in the exact colors above — this guarantees crisp, readable text every time, which AI models often botch on the first pass.
- **Reference the actual Book 1 cover file** if your image tool supports image-to-image or "style reference" uploads — `CulinaryCrescendo_frontcover_google.pdf` (or a PNG export of it) as a style guide will get you much closer than text alone.
