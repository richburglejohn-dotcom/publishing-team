## Why the voice breaks here on purpose
Every other piece of Baron content — bios, scripts, captions — stays locked in his voice, per the series bible rule ("never change the author's voice"). This is the one deliberate exception, and it's meant to be recognizable as one: a short, out-of-character author's note, not Baron explaining himself. Readers trust a real "here's what this is" from Lejohn more than they'd trust an in-character disclosure from Baron, which would just read as one more layer of the bit.
