Creole Crab & Botanical Guacamole. Signature starter at the Apothecary / Bayouboujee.

---

## Full Recipe

**Yield:** Serves 2–4 (Appetizer) | **Prep time:** 15 minutes | **Difficulty:** Moderate

### The Creole Elixir (Crab Marinade)
- 8 oz Jumbo Lump Crabmeat (picked for shells, keep lumps intact)
- 1 Tbsp high-quality extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp fresh lime juice
- 1/4 tsp Creole seasoning (house blend or Tony Chachere's)
- 3 dashes Peychaud's Bitters

### The Botanical Base
- 3 ripe Hass avocados
- 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
- 1 Tbsp pickled jalapeño brine (the acid secret)
- 1/4 cup red onion, very finely minced
- 1 fresh Fresno chili, seeded and minced (substitute jalapeño for less color)
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, roughly chopped
- 1 Tbsp fresh chives, finely snipped
- Kosher salt and fresh cracked black pepper, to taste

### The Finish
- Garnish: sliced radish, micro-cilantro, purple chive blossoms, smoked paprika
- Vessel: dark ceramic mortar or heavy bowl
- Serve with: house-made plantain chips or garlic-rubbed crostini

### Method
1. **Prepare the Elixir** — whisk olive oil, 1 tsp lime juice, Creole seasoning, and Peychaud's Bitters until emulsified.
2. **Infuse the crab** — gently fold crabmeat into the elixir, once or twice, without breaking the lumps. Marinate 5 minutes.
3. **Mash the base** — scoop avocado flesh, immediately add 2 Tbsp lime juice and jalapeño brine to stop oxidation, season with kosher salt. Mash with a fork — chunky, not puréed.
4. **Fold the aromatics** — add red onion, Fresno chili, cilantro, chives. Fold gently. Taste and adjust.
5. **The marriage** — fold in about half the marinated crab (with juices). Fold gently to incorporate.
6. **Plate** — spoon into the serving vessel, create a shallow well in the center, pile the remaining crab lumps high. Garnish with radish, microgreens/flowers, smoked paprika. Serve immediately.

## Kitchen Station Card (Line Version)

**Station:** Garde Manger / Cold Apps | **Allergens:** Shellfish, Allium, Citrus | **Shelf life:** Guac made to order / marinade 3 days

**A. Crab Marinade (batch, in squeeze bottle):** 1 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup fresh lime juice, 1 Tbsp Creole seasoning, 1 Tbsp Peychaud's Bitters. Combine and shake until emulsified.

**B. Guac Veg Mix (batch, in 1/6 pan — no lime or salt here):** 1 cup red onion (fine mince), 4 Fresno chilies (seeded, minced), 1 cup cilantro (chopped), 1/4 cup chives (snipped). Toss, refrigerate.

**C. Crab Portioning:** 8 oz jumbo lump per order, picked for shells, kept dry.

**Per-order execution:**
1. Mash 3 avocados + 2 Tbsp lime juice + 1 Tbsp jalapeño brine + kosher salt. Leave chunky.
2. Fold in ~1/2 cup Guac Veg Mix. Taste for salt.
3. Dress 8 oz crab with 1 Tbsp marinade. Fold once, gently — do not break lumps.
4. Fold half the dressed crab into the guacamole. Spoon into chilled mortar bowl, well in the center.
5. Pile remaining crab in the well (height matters). Garnish: 3 fanned radish slices, micro-cilantro/flowers, smoked paprika dust.
6. Serve with plantain chips.

**Chef notes:** Watch waste on jumbo lump — don't over-mix or it turns stringy. Don't pre-mash avocados more than 15 minutes before service (oxidation). If marinade runs out, never skip the Peychaud's — it's the signature flavor.
