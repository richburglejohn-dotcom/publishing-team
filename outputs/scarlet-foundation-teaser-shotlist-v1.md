## Shot List

### Shot 1 — Kitchen Portrait (Storyboard panel 1)
- **Time:** 0:00–0:03 (3s) — THE HOOK
- **Frame:** Medium, straight to camera. Baron in grey chef coat, kitchen behind him, steam rising off the line. Low, moody light — let the kitchen glow behind him, face half-lit.
- **Action:** Baron holds the lens. No blinking, no smile.
- **VO:** "I don't usually explain myself."
- **Burned-in text:** none — the line carries it. (Auto-captions ON.)

### Shot 2 — Close-Up (Storyboard panel 2)
- **Time:** 0:03–0:06 (3s)
- **Frame:** Tight close-up, eyes just off-lens. Warm side-light.
- **Action:** A slow half-turn toward camera on the last word.
- **VO:** "But since this is a first post — I'll make an exception."
- **Burned-in text:** none.

### Shot 3 — Hands / Knife Work (Storyboard panel 3)
- **Time:** 0:06–0:13 (7s)
- **Frame:** Overhead-angle insert: hands dicing shallots, morning light. Real knife rhythm — the sound matters, keep natural audio under the text.
- **Action:** Continuous chopping. Text lands one line at a time, timed to the knife.
- **VO:** none — knife sounds only.
- **Burned-in text (one line per beat):**
  1. "Sous chef. French Quarter."
  2. "No restaurant yet."
  3. "LLC's filed. Domain's live."

### Shot 4 — The Gravel Lot, Wide (Storyboard panel 4)
- **Time:** 0:13–0:18 (5s)
- **Frame:** Wide. Baron small in frame, centered, standing on the empty gravel lot, Creole townhouses behind. Golden hour.
- **Action:** He doesn't move. The lot does the talking.
- **VO:** none until the beat lands.
- **Burned-in text:** "Gravel lot off Magazine Street nobody gave me permission to build on."
- **VO (flat, certain, at 0:16):** "I wasn't asking."

### Shot 5 — The Folder (Storyboard panel 5)
- **Time:** 0:18–0:21 (3s)
- **Frame:** Medium, same lot, sun flaring behind him. Baron holds the manila folder (the LLC filing) at chest height — presenting it without presenting it.
- **Action:** One small lift of the folder. Nothing else.
- **VO:** none. Silence is the beat.
- **Burned-in text:** none.

### Shot 6 — Title Card (Storyboard panel 6)
- **Time:** 0:21–0:25 (4s)
- **Frame:** Black card, white serif type (Playfair Display to match the brand).
- **Text, stacked exactly as storyboarded:**
  "Baron Vieux."
  "Follow."
  "This is the beginning."
- **Audio:** Cut all ambient. Either full silence or one low sustained note.

## Audio Notes
- No music bed until Shot 6, if at all — natural kitchen/street ambience reads more premium and matches the "no fluff" brand.
- Record VO separately from the footage; Baron's delivery is deadpan, unhurried, never salesy.
- Burned-in captions are mandatory — most viewers watch muted.
