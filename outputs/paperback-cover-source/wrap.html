THE KALDI’S SERIES  ·  PREQUEL
He didn’t find his calling.
He built it from scratch, one shift at a time.

Baron Vieux has run corporate sales floors, managed restaurants, and survived a pandemic that erased everything he built. Now he’s sous chef in someone else’s kitchen, executing someone else’s vision, while a signature reduction simmers on his own stove at home.

One insult from the wrong man on the wrong night changes the math.

The LLC is already filed. The domain is already live. And a reduction of roasted tomato, smoked guajillo, and Peychaud’s Bitters — never written down, belonging to no one but him — is about to become the Scarlet Elixir.

This is how Bayouboujee gets born. New Orleans as witness. Ownership as the only ending that matters.

The prequel to Culinary Crescendo: A Symphony of Love in the Big Easy.

Lejohn Richburg III
