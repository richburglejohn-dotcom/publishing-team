### Script 2 — "Never Written Down" (The Elixir)
**Duration:** 35 seconds
**Hook style:** Process/ASMR-adjacent, ends on a turn

HOOK (0–4 sec):
[Close shot: tomato roasting, guajillo charring over an electric coil]
TEXT: "Four months of days off. One reduction."

VOICE:
"Roasted tomato. Smoked guajillo. Worcestershire. Peychaud's. Dark roux — from scratch, because the shortcut tastes like a shortcut."

CUT: [Reduction simmering, thickening]

VOICE:
"Reduced. Then reduced again."

BEAT (1 sec, silence)

VOICE:
"I never wrote it down."

TEXT ON SCREEN: "Some recipes you pass down. This one's mine."

CTA: "Follow for more from the line."

VOICE:
