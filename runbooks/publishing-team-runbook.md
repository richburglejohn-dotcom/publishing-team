# Publishing Team Runbook
## Culinary Crescendo — FuturIQ

This document explains how to use your AI publishing team.
Read this before starting any major task.

---

## Your Agent Team

| Agent | Job | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| `manuscript-editor` | Grammar, punctuation, spelling, prose quality | After writing any chapter or scene |
| `continuity-checker` | Makes sure the prequel doesn't contradict the published book | Before finalizing any prequel chapter |
| `chapter-developer` | Writes or expands scenes in the author's voice | When you need a scene drafted or expanded |
| `book-blurb-writer` | Back cover copy, short pitches, taglines | Before uploading to Amazon or sharing the book |
| `amazon-listing-optimizer` | Full Amazon KDP listing: title, blurb, keywords, categories | Before publishing or updating on Amazon |
| `launch-sequence-planner` | Full book launch timeline and task calendar | 8+ weeks before launch day |
| `email-marketing-agent` | Email sequences, newsletters, reader follow-ups | Building or nurturing your reader list |
| `social-media-content-agent` | Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok posts | Ongoing content and launch support |
| `content-angle-strategist` | YouTube titles, hooks, video outlines | Creating author content on YouTube |
| `market-signal-researcher` | Research, audience analysis, opportunity scoring | Before making a strategy decision |
| `cover-designer-architect` | Cover design briefs and direction | Before working with a designer or AI image tool |

---

## Workflows

### Workflow 1 — Finishing the Prequel

**Goal:** Complete and polish *The Scarlet Foundation*

1. Use `chapter-developer` to draft or expand any incomplete scenes
2. Use `manuscript-editor` to clean each chapter
3. Use `continuity-checker` to verify each chapter against the published book
4. Repeat until manuscript is complete

### Workflow 2 — Launching the Prequel

**Goal:** Publish *The Scarlet Foundation* on Amazon and create a launch event

1. Use `book-blurb-writer` to create back cover copy and tagline
2. Use `amazon-listing-optimizer` to create the full KDP listing
3. Use `cover-designer-architect` to brief the cover design
4. Use `launch-sequence-planner` to create the launch calendar
5. Use `email-marketing-agent` to write the pre-launch and launch email sequences
6. Use `social-media-content-agent` to create launch week content
7. Use `content-angle-strategist` to plan a YouTube video around the launch

### Workflow 3 — Ongoing Marketing

**Goal:** Build audience, sell books, grow the FuturIQ brand

1. Use `market-signal-researcher` to identify content and offer opportunities
2. Use `content-angle-strategist` to turn opportunities into YouTube content
3. Use `social-media-content-agent` for weekly social posts
4. Use `email-marketing-agent` for monthly newsletter content

## How to Start a Task

In any Claude Code conversation, simply describe what you need.

**Examples:**
- "Edit Chapter 3 of the prequel for grammar and punctuation"
- "Write the Amazon blurb for The Scarlet Foundation"
- "Create a 5-email welcome sequence for new readers"
- "Plan the launch for the prequel — target date is [date]"
- "Give me 10 Instagram posts for launch week"

The right agent will activate automatically based on your request.

## Rules for the Team

1. Always read `references/series-bible.md` before starting any task
2. Never change the author's voice — match it
3. The published book is the authority — the prequel must not contradict it
4. Every output must connect to the reader, a sale, or the next step
5. No filler. No generic advice. No "journey."

---

## After Each Major Task
Save completed work to the `outputs/` folder with a clear filename.
